How to Play

  • Tap Start Game to begin your adventure!
  • Move left/right with arrow keys or on-screen buttons.
  • Jump with the up arrow, spacebar, or jump button.
  • Collect coins for points and try to beat your high score!
  • Avoid logs and obstacles. If you hit one, the game ends.
  • Play again and have fun!
