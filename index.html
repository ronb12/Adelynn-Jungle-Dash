How to Play

🎮 How to Play

  • Arrow Keys or WASD to move left/right
  • Space or Up Arrow to jump
  • Down Arrow or S to slide
  • Q for left wall run, E for right wall run
  • Swipe Up to jump, Swipe Down to slide
  • Swipe Left/Right to move lanes

🎯 What to Do

  • 🏃‍♀️ Run as far as you can!
  • 🪙 Collect coins for points
  • 🚫 Avoid obstacles and gaps
  • 🔥 Slide under fire traps
  • 🧱 Wall run to avoid walls
  • ⚡ Grab power-ups for special abilities

⭐ Special Features

  • 🪙 Magnet: Attracts coins from a distance
  • 🛡️ Shield: Protects against obstacles and fire
  • 🗣️ Talking Character: Adelynn talks to you!
  • 🌍 Language Learning: Learn some Spanish words

🚧 Obstacles

  • 🕳️ Gaps: Jump over them
  • 🧱 Walls: Wall run to avoid them
  • 🔥 Fire Traps: Slide under them
  • 🚫 Regular Obstacles: Jump or move around them

🏆 Scoring

  • Coins give points based on combo
  • Longer runs = higher scores
  • Try to beat your high score!

💡 Tip: Use power-ups strategically to maximize your score!
